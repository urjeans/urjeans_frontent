About Us

URJEANS

The foundation of Urjeans

URJEANS was founded in 2017 with a clear mission — to craft premium denim products that meet the evolving standards of global fashion. What started as a focused operation has grown into a dedicated jeans manufacturing company with a sharp eye for detail and quality.

Today, URJEANS operates two full-scale production lines, equipped with modern machinery and a skilled workforce committed to consistent excellence.

With the capacity to produce up to 4,000 pairs of jeans per day, we are proud to serve local and international clients seeking reliability, durability, and craftsmanship. From classic cuts to modern designs, every product reflects our passion for denim.

As we continue to grow, URJEANS remains rooted in quality, stability, and responsibility — the core values that shape every stitch we sew.

OUR DENIM PRODUCTION PROCESS

Where craftsmanship meets precision at every stage.

Step 1 – Automated Marking & Measurement

Smart Marking for Efficiency

Using digitized systems, we mark each piece for seam alignment, placement, and component cutting.

Step 2 – Cutting the Fabric

Precision in Every Pattern

Our production begins with high-accuracy cutting machines to ensure consistent sizing across every pair of jeans.

Step 3 – Sewing & Assembly

Expert Stitching for Long-Lasting Wear

Our skilled team operates advanced industrial sewing machines to join denim pieces with reinforced seams.

Step 4 – Finishing & Folding

Final Touches for a Perfect Look

Before packaging, every pair undergoes a final inspection for quality control.
